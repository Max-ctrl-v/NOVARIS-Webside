§ 2 Subject Matter

NOVARIS provides consulting services in the area of the R&D Tax Credit under the German Research Allowance Act (FZulG). The exact scope of services is determined by the individual proposal or service description.

The services include in particular:

  • Analysis and identification of eligible research and development projects
  • Preparation and submission of the certification application to the Certification Body for Research Allowance (BSFZ)
  • Support with the application to the responsible tax office
  • Guidance and communication during the certification process
  • Documentation of R&D activities in accordance with FZulG requirements

NOVARIS does not owe any specific outcome (in particular not the approval of the R&D Tax Credit), but rather the professional delivery of the agreed consulting services.

§ 3 Fees and Payment Terms

3.1 Success-Based Fee

Unless otherwise agreed in the individual contract, NOVARIS's fee is success-based. The fee only becomes due when the Client has received a positive certification from the BSFZ and/or an R&D Tax Credit notice from the tax office.

3.2 Fee Amount

The exact fee amount (percentage of the approved R&D Tax Credit or fixed price) is specified in the individual proposal.

3.3 Due Date

Invoices are due for payment within 14 days of the invoice date without deduction, unless otherwise agreed.

3.4 Value Added Tax

All prices are quoted exclusive of the applicable statutory value added tax.
