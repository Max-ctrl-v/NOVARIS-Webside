Engineering / Technical Services

Engineering Firm Secures EUR 620,000 Through Structured Application

Company (Anonymized)

Specialized engineering firm for structural analysis and material testing, approx. 65 employees, Bavaria

Challenge
  • Development of novel testing methods for additive manufacturing components (3D-printed parts)
  • As an SME with 65 employees, eligible for the 35% funding rate
  • Limited administrative capacity – management wanted to fully delegate the process
