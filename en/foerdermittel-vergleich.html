The Most Important Funding Programs for Research and Development

Germany is one of the countries worldwide with the most comprehensive government research and development funding. Companies – from innovative startups to established corporations – can choose from a wide variety of funding programs at federal, state, and EU level. The challenge lies not in a lack of options, but in identifying the right programs, submitting applications correctly, and optimally combining funding.

In this comprehensive comparison, we present the four most important national and European R&D funding programs: the R&D Tax Credit (FZulG), the Central Innovation Program for SMEs (ZIM), KMU-innovativ of the BMBF, and the EIC Accelerator of the European Union. Each of these programs has specific strengths, target groups, and requirements.

The R&D Tax Credit deserves special mention: As the only program, it provides a statutory legal entitlement to funding – regardless of industry, company size, or competition. Since the 2024 amendment, the assessment basis has been raised to EUR 12 million, corresponding to a maximum allowance of up to EUR 4.2 million per year for SMEs. Additionally, the R&D Tax Credit can be applied for retroactively from the year 2020 – a decisive advantage for companies that have not previously used funding.

Forschungszulage (FZulG)

Tax-Based R&D Funding with Legal Entitlement

The R&D Tax Credit under the Research Allowance Act (FZulG) is the most accessible R&D funding program in Germany. Since its introduction in 2020, it has established itself as the most important instrument of tax-based research funding. The decisive advantage: there is a statutory legal entitlement to the allowance – if your project meets the criteria, you are guaranteed to receive the funding.

All Company Sizes – vom Startup bis zum Konzern

ZIM – Zentrales Innovationsprogramm Mittelstand

Project-Based Funding for SMEs

ZIM is one of the most successful and well-known funding programs for small and medium-sized enterprises in Germany. It is aimed at SMEs with fewer than 500 employees and supports innovative individual projects, cooperation projects, and innovation networks. The funding rate is up to 60% of eligible costs – depending on company size and project type.

Unlike the R&D Tax Credit, there is no legal entitlement with ZIM: approval is competitive, and the quality of the application is decisive. In return, ZIM covers a broader spectrum of costs – in addition to personnel costs, also materials, equipment, and external research contracts. Processing time is typically 4 to 6 months. ZIM is particularly suitable for SMEs with clearly defined, technologically demanding development projects.

KMU-innovativ (BMBF)

Promoting Cutting-Edge Research in SMEs

KMU-innovativ is a funding initiative of the Federal Ministry of Education and Research (BMBF), specifically designed for high-risk, technologically demanding research projects of small and medium-sized enterprises. The program features a simplified, accelerated application process – a response to criticism that SMEs are disadvantaged by the complexity of traditional BMBF funding procedures.

Funding is topic-specific in clearly defined technology fields such as biotechnology, medical technology, information and communication technology, production technology, materials research, electronics, and photonics. KMU-innovativ offers up to 60% funding rate for individual projects and covers personnel, material, and travel costs as well as subcontracts. The key difference from ZIM: KMU-innovativ focuses on cutting-edge research in defined technology fields and offers particularly fast evaluation procedures.

EIC Accelerator (EU)

European Top-Tier Funding for Scale-ups

The EIC Accelerator is the flagship funding program of the European Innovation Council, targeting highly innovative companies with scaling potential. It combines a non-repayable grant of up to EUR 2.5 million with an optional equity investment of up to EUR 15 million – a unique funding construct in Europe.

The program is highly competitive: only about 5-8% of applicants receive funding. The application is submitted at EU level through the European Commission (EISMEA) and involves a multi-stage evaluation including a personal interview before an international jury. Processing time from submission to approval is 6 to 12 months. The EIC Accelerator is particularly suitable for companies with disruptive technologies and international scaling potential.

Kombinationsstrategie

Combining Funding Programs Smartly

Maximize your funding through the intelligent combination of different programs.

Das Grundprinzip: No Double Funding

The most important rule when combining funding programs: The same costs may not be funded twice. However, this does not mean you can only use one program. On the contrary – a smart allocation of cost items makes it possible to use multiple programs in parallel and significantly increase total funding.

The R&D Tax Credit is particularly flexible: it can be combined with almost all other funding programs. The key factor is that only those costs that are not already funded by another program should be included in the assessment basis for the R&D Tax Credit. In practice, this means: use ZIM or KMU-innovativ for direct project costs, and apply for the R&D Tax Credit for the remaining, uncovered personnel costs.

Which Funding Program Is Right for You?

Find the optimal funding strategy for your company in just a few steps.

You Are a Large Enterprise?

The R&D Tax Credit is your best option. As the only program, it is also open to large enterprises – with legal entitlement, without competition. Check retroactively from 2020 all R&D personnel costs. With an assessment basis of up to EUR 12M, you receive up to EUR 3M allowance per year.

Empfehlung: Forschungszulage

Sie sind KMU mit definiertem F&E-Projekt?

Combine ZIM + R&D Tax Credit for maximum funding. ZIM offers higher project funding (up to 60%), while the R&D Tax Credit covers the remaining personnel costs. This way you achieve a total funding rate of well over 40% of your total R&D costs.

Empfehlung: ZIM + Forschungszulage

Sie forschen in einem BMBF-Technologiefeld?

Consider KMU-innovativ as a first step. The program offers fast evaluation and high funding rates for cutting-edge research in defined technology fields (biotech, medtech, ICT, manufacturing, etc.). Supplement with the R&D Tax Credit for unfunded personnel costs.

Empfehlung: KMU-innovativ + Forschungszulage

Sie wollen international skalieren?

The EIC Accelerator + R&D Tax Credit is the strongest combination for scale-ups with disruptive technologies. The EIC offers up to EUR 17.5M (grant + equity), and the R&D Tax Credit supplements national R&D funding. The effort is high, but the potential is enormous.

Empfehlung: EIC Accelerator + Forschungszulage
